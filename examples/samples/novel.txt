# Novel Sample - Chapter 1 Excerpt

The morning sun filtered through the rice paper screens, casting long shadows across the tatami floor. Sakura sat in seiza position, her kimono arranged with meticulous care around her.

「お茶をお持ちしました」

The servant's voice broke the silence. She entered without looking up, carrying a lacquered tray with practiced grace.

彼女は庭を見つめながら、過去の記憶に思いを馳せた。
あの夏の日、二人で見た夕焼けを今でも覚えている。

"Thank you," Sakura replied softly, watching the steam rise from the cup.

桜は茶碗を両手で受け取り、一口飲んだ。苦味の中に微かな甘みを感じた。

The bitter tea reminded her of her grandmother's words:
「人生の苦しみは、いつか甘く変わるものです」

Life's bitterness will one day turn sweet.
